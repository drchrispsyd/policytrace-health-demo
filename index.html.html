PolicyTrace

Explainable Clinical Governance Review
Supporting expert judgment—not replacing it

Review policy through evidence, reasoning, and measurement intelligence.

Every rating, strength, gap, and recommendation must show its basis in the submitted policy. Unsupported claims are withheld.

Evidence rule: PolicyTrace will not produce a rating, finding, strength, or recommendation unless it can display the policy evidence and explain the reasoning supporting it.
Document intake boundary: this build intentionally uses verified pasted text or a plain-text file. PDF/OCR ingestion is treated as separate infrastructure so it cannot silently undermine the validity of the governance review.
Run a governance review to generate a traceable report.

Measurement Intelligence

This library supports expert selection of empirically supported tools. It explains potential fit, limitations, implementation burden, and intended use; it does not prescribe a measure without context.

PolicyTrace Review Standard

Purpose. Support qualified healthcare professionals in evaluating, strengthening, and defending policy decisions through transparent reasoning.

1Quote exact policy language.
2Explain what the language accomplishes.
3Identify a precise gap or strength.
4Explain why it matters.
5Offer editable alternative language.
6Explain why the alternative is stronger.
Basis of review: outputs are based solely on the submitted text and the transparent rules shown in the report. They do not establish legal, regulatory, accreditation, or clinical compliance and do not replace professional judgment, legal review, local data, or current source verification.
